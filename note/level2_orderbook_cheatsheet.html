Signals to Watch Click a signal to simulate ⚡
Big bid wall below price — heavy buying support; protects downside, making it safer to enter long positions.
Big ask wall above price — heavy selling resistance; wait for buyers to clear it before chasing breakouts.
Ask wall disappears / clears — buying volume completely eats the wall, causing a rapid breakout surge.
Bid wall disappears / cracks — selling volume breaches the support, resulting in a sudden panic drop.
Wall vanishes as price nears it — fake orders placed to manipulate price and canceled right before fills.
