Level 2 order book — quick reference

Bid side (buyers)
What it showsPrices buyers will pay
DirectionPrices go down ↓
Big order = Support / floor
Stacking fast =Bullish
Ask side (sellers)
What it showsPrices sellers accept
DirectionPrices go up ↑
Big order =Resistance / ceiling
Stacking fast =Bearish
The spread
FormulaAsk − Bid
Tight (<$0.10)Liquid
Wide (>$1.00)Risky
Cost of market order= spread
Signals to watch
Big bid wall below price — downside protected, safer to buy
Big ask wall above price — wait for it to break before entering
Ask wall disappears — price often surges, possible breakout
Bid wall disappears — price may drop fast, possible breakdown
Wall vanishes as price nears it — likely spoofing, be cautious
Limitations
Dark pool trades are hidden · Data changes every millisecond · Always combine with chart + volume · Best for short-term / day trading, not long-term investing
